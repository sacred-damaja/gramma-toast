how to make gramma toast

  1. preheat oven to 350 spread out slices of bread on a cookie sheet typically 1 to 2 slices per person
  2. butter all slices then dust well with sugar and cinnamon do this step twice
  3. next drizzle evaporated or whole milk in the center of the bread bake 12-15minutes or until toasted and brown on the bottom my mom would always serve with tea
